## STAKES & PRESSURE ANALYSIS

### GP-001: Dual-Layer Stakes ✓ COMPLIANT

**Physical Stakes**:
- Margot physically trapped in Gardens (escape impossible due to security + Deadman's Switch)
- Blood requirement creates ongoing danger (Celeste's hunger every 72 hours)
- Cure attempt failure nearly kills Margot (Ch 15-16)

**Psychological/Moral Stakes**:
- Margot's moral descent: captive → collaborator → willing accomplice
- Complicity in sustaining vampire (blood supply assistance)
- Identity erosion (who is she becoming?)

**Assessment**: ✓ Both layers present and active throughout. Physical danger establishes urgency; moral corruption provides deeper stakes.
