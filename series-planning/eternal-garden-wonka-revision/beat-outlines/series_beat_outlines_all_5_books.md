# Book 1: The Eternal Garden

**Premise:** A professional thief and her crew break into a vampire inventor's underground steampunk wonderland for a hired job‚Äîbut Celeste's deliberate test eliminates them one by one. The sole survivor, Margot, faces an impossible choice: become keeper to a serial killer with a deadman's switch, or let weapon designs destroy London.

**Timeline:** 1885, 4 months
**Word Count:** ~100,000 (~25 chapters, ~4,000 words each)
**Structure:** 40% horror opening (Wonka test) + 30% impossible choice + 20% partnership development + 10% choice to stay
**Romance Stage:** Terror ‚Üí Survival ‚Üí Fascination (no intimate scenes, tension/unwanted attraction only)
**Cure Progress:** 0% ‚Üí 5% (first attempt shows promise but fails, data valuable)

## Act I: The Tour of Death (Chapters 1-10, ~40%)

### Beat 1: Opening Image / Ordinary World
**Steampunk Thread (60%):**
- Celeste alone in the Hartwell Mechanical Gardens (her creation, built 1872-1875)
- 10 years isolated, working on cure Device #1 (mechanical counter-resonance to vampirism)
- Blood delivery from Augustus (monthly, controlled supply)
- Her motivation: "I'm just solving the most interesting problem I've ever encountered"
- Flashback: built this place as magnum opus cage after 12 murders (1870-1872)
- Deadman's switch installed 1880: if she dies, weapon designs release to London criminals

**Romance Thread (40%):**
- Celeste's belief: "I'm incapable of love. I'm a psychopath who became a vampire‚Äîamplification of emptiness."
- Expects to die alone in her cage
- No human contact except Augustus in 10 years

**Emotional State:** Isolated, clinical, brilliant, monstrous, resigned

**Chapter 1 Setup:** Establish Celeste's routine, Gardens mechanics, cure obsession, deadman's switch importance (prevents Court/Van Helsing from killing her)

### Beat 2: Inciting Incident ‚Äì The Hired Job
**Steampunk Thread (Ch 2-3):**
- **Professional crew of 5 thieves breaks in** (hired job: kidnap Celeste for ransom OR steal cure invention)
- Crew composition: Leader (brute strength), Demolitions expert, Safecracker, Lookout, **Margot (locks/mechanical systems expert)**

**Romance Thread:**

**Emotional State:** Professional confidence (crew) ‚Üí rising dread as doors seal

**Chapters 2-3:** Break-in sequence, lockdown triggers, crew realizes they're trapped, first glimpse of Celeste in shadows

### Beat 3: The Wonka Test Begins (Ch 4-7, ~28%)

**Chapters 4-7:** Four crew deaths (escalating horror), Margot's survival moment, Celeste reveals herself fully, recognition: "You'll do."

### Beat 4: Augustus's Pitch (Ch 8-10, ~12%)
**Steampunk Thread:**
- Augustus arrives (monthly blood delivery), finds Margot alive and crew dead
- **The Impossible Stakes Revealed (Chapter 8):**
  - **Deadman's switch explained:** Celeste's death releases weapon designs (steam-powered bombs, pneumatic assassination devices) to London's criminal underworld

  - Option C: Report to authorities ‚Üí Deadman's switch activates, London's criminals get weapons
