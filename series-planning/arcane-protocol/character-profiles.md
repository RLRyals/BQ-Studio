# ARCANE PROTOCOL - Character Profiles

## Main Characters

### Malachite "Mal" Davies (POV Character)

**Role:** Rookie Runner (forensic technician), police department's first mage hire

**Physical Description:**
- [To be developed during detailed planning]

**Background:**
- Daughter of The Darkness (notorious serial killer of unlicensed half-dragons)
- Turned in her mother as a child
- Extensive magical knowledge from mother's "training" (purpose unknown until Book 5)
- Rejected magical practice after mother's arrest
- Built identity on NOT being her mother

**Core Traits:**
- Competent, professional, detail-oriented
- Extensive theoretical magical knowledge
- Trust issues (laws are reliable; people betray)
- Avoids emotional connections
- Focused on moving forward, not backward
- Represses traumatic memories

**Internal Conflict:**
- Knowledge vs. practice (has expertise but refuses to use magic)
- Past vs. present (everyone wants her to remember; she wants to forget)
- Professional duty vs. personal trauma (solving cases connected to mother's legacy)
- Fear of becoming her mother

**Magical Capabilities:**
- Theoretical knowledge of rituals, enchantments, supernatural creatures
- Can IDENTIFY magical components and ritual structures
- Can USE pre-enchanted objects
- Can DESIGN ritual structures
- CANNOT perform enchantments without power source (Ethereal Dragon or half-dragon partner)
- By Book 5: Competent enchanter with appropriate power sources

**5-Book Arc:**

**Book 1 - Competent But Limited:**
- Solves copycat case using theoretical knowledge alone
- Refuses to use pre-enchanted tracking object
- Loses critical evidence to Church retrieval (professional consequence)
- Represses memories when asked about sword

**Book 2 - First Practical Use:**
- Forced to design enchanted tracking device (professional necessity)
- Works with half-dragon partner (she designs, they power)
- Enrolls in night classes (Simone's pressure: "fix it or find new career")
- Frustrated by systemic barriers
- Dreams intensify (repressed memories bleeding through)

**Book 3 - Student & Professional:**
- Attending classes with Perry Keene
- Forms cautious working relationships with classmates
- Graduates Runner training mid-book
- Uses classroom knowledge to identify ritual components
- Nightmares worsen; refuses therapy/hypnosis
- Everyone pushing her to remember; she actively resists

**Book 4 - Competent Practitioner:**
- Works as equal with Rhys (no training wheels)
- Competent enchanter with half-dragon power sources
- Realizes sect wants her because of mother's unique training
- Can identify sect enchantments by recognizing mother's technique variations
- Repressed memories causing panic attacks, flashbacks
- Realizes she might have to face past but not ready yet

**Book 5 - Facing Legacy:**
- Forced to remember (sect's magical coercion)
- Recovery is traumatic, not triumphant
- Remembers sword location, mother's true purpose for her
- Uses mother's knowledge to destroy sword and stop Rite
- Finds peace: can't escape past, only choose what to do with it
- Proves she's not her mother by using knowledge to protect

**Relationships:**
- **Rhys:** Antagonist → investigator → ally → lover (slow progression)
- **Jax:** Mentor → trusted partner → complicated (his secrets)
- **Simone:** Authority figure with questionable motives
- **Perry:** Professor, mentor in magical practice
- **Classmates:** Found family outside police work

### Federal Agent Rhys Kazemi

**Role:** Federal investigator assigned to supernatural crimes, searching for The Darkness's sword

**Physical Description:**
- [To be developed during detailed planning]

**Background:**
- Federal office originally in DC
- Assigned to Oakridge to investigate copycat killings
- Transfers permanently to Oakridge office (Book 2)
- Access to federal resources unavailable to local PD

**Core Traits:**
- Intelligent, thorough investigator
- Initially suspicious and judgmental
- Struggles with attraction vs. professional duty
- Acts like jerk when fighting feelings
- Capable of admitting when wrong (but takes time)

**Internal Conflict:**
- Duty vs. attraction (investigating someone he's drawn to)
- Assumptions vs. reality (realizes he was wrong about Mal)
- Career advancement vs. personal happiness
- Protecting Mal from federal scrutiny while also investigating her

**Investigation Focus:**
- Book 1: Believes Mal is copycat or has sword
- Book 2: Treats Mal as asset to find sword and sect
- Book 3: Realizes sect thinks she knows sword location; she's in danger
- Book 4: Accepts she doesn't consciously know; respects boundaries
- Book 5: Supports her when forced to remember

**5-Book Arc:**

**Book 1 - Full Antagonist:**
- Arrives suspecting Mal is copycat killer
- Evidence: access to knowledge, trained by serial killer, ritual expertise
- "Where's the sword? You know where it is."
- By end: Realizes she turned mother in, actively rejects legacy
- Still suspects she has/knows about sword

**Book 2 - Investigative Asset:**
- Transfers to Oakridge permanently (official: sect case; real: watching Mal)
- Treats her as lead to sect and sword
- Distant, dismissive, takes notes constantly
- "If you help me find it, federal protection from sect"
- She refuses: "I'm solving murders, not treasure hunting"
- Realizes: Sect thinks she knows where sword is

**Book 3 - Fighting Feelings:**
- Forced proximity during joint federal/local investigation
- Acts like jerk to maintain emotional distance
- Criticizes methods, questions judgment, undermines her
- She calls him out: "You don't trust me? Then leave."
- He can't leave (needs her for case)
- Starting to recognize pushing her to remember is violation

**Book 4 - Earned Respect:**
- Treats her as equal investigator
- Working relationship solid
- No longer pushing about sword
- One honest moment: "I was wrong about you. I'm sorry."
- She doesn't forgive easily
- Personal feelings still complicated
- Stops treating her as suspect/asset

**Book 5 - Romance Begins:**
- Allies working together to stop Rite
- Supports her when forced to remember (doesn't push, just present)
- Moderate steam scenes
- Together by book end but relationship new, fragile
- No grand declarations: "Let's see where this goes"

**Relationships:**
- **Mal:** Antagonist → asset → jerk → ally → lover
- **Jax:** Professional respect; federal/local cooperation
- **Church:** Frustrated by jurisdiction blocks
- **Guild:** Investigating corruption at Council level

### Senior Runner Jax Spencer

**Role:** Mal's mentor, secretly half-dragon with connections to criminal uncle Oz

**Physical Description:**
- [To be developed during detailed planning]
- Eyes change to dragon slits when using truth-compelling ability

**Background:**
- Half-dragon (secret from most people)
- One ability: truth-compelling (only works on humans)
- Nephew of criminal kingpin Oz
- Pretends to be human to avoid Guild licensing
- Pays Church tithe as part of human cover
- Has private AI (illegal; others don't know)

**Core Traits:**
- Cold professional exterior
- Maintains precarious double life
- Connected to both law enforcement and criminal networks
- Protective of his secrets
- Experiences tech-induced symptoms (removes AR glasses frequently)

**Internal Conflict:**
- Law enforcement vs. criminal family loyalty
- Hiding supernatural nature while working as cop
- Protecting unlicensed half-dragons vs. enforcing Guild laws
- Relationship with nephew Lark (hate each other since childhood)

**Supernatural Limitations:**
- Truth-compelling only works on humans (not other supernaturals)
- Eyes visibly change to dragon slits when using ability
- Cannot use ability if being observed
- Technology causes supernatural sensitivity (can't admit migraines)
- Must maintain human cover or face Guild licensing

**Technology Behavior:**
- AR glasses: Removes frequently (supernatural tech sensitivity)
- Cannot use "migraine" as excuse (would expose supernatural nature)
- Pattern noticeable to Mal by Book 2
- Uses private AI extensively (Mal notices "talking to himself")

**5-Book Arc:**

**Book 1 - Cold Mentor:**
- Training Mal as Runner
- Professional, no warmth
- Close call: dragon eyes visible during witness interview
- Mal notices something odd but can't identify it
- Uses private AI; Mal sees him talking to "himself"
- No protective moments, just doing job

**Book 2 - Connections Revealed:**
- Half-dragon community contacts him
- Confirms victim was part of Oz's network
- AR glasses pattern obvious (removes them regularly)
- Mal gets suspicious: Connected to Oz? Half-dragon? Trustworthy?
- No emotional moments, just information exchange

**Book 3 - Transitioning Out:**
- Mal graduates training, gets new partner
- Becomes side character
- Appears with information from "sources" (Oz's network)
- Nearly exposed during raid (someone sees dragon eyes)
- Mal confronts him about rumors; he stonewalls
- Trust fractures: Is he using her? Reporting to Oz?

**Book 4 - Information Source:**
- Side character status continues
- Appears with warnings from Oz's network
- Sect threatens Oz's operations (killing his clients)
- Reveals limited information to protect Mal
- Introduces Lark (makes clear they hate each other)

**Book 5 - Secret Nearly Exposed:**
- Nearly revealed during final battle
- Multiple witnesses see dragon eyes
- Lark uses memory-alteration magic (despite hating Jax, protects him)
- Mal suspects but has no proof
- Ends with detective promotion, partnered with Mal again
- Sets up Series 2: both keeping secrets from Simone

**Relationships:**
- **Mal:** Mentor → partner → complicated (secrets between them)
- **Oz:** Uncle; criminal connections; complicated loyalty
- **Lark:** Cousin; hate each other since childhood
- **Half-dragon community:** Connected through Oz's network
- **Church:** Maintains cover through tithe payments

**Series 2 Role:**
- Co-protagonist with Mal
- Church worldbuilding focus (his tithe payments, preservation politics)
- Secret becomes central conflict
- Partnership with Mal under Simone's surveillance

## Supporting Characters

### The Darkness (Mal's Mother)

**Role:** Serial killer, Mal's trauma source, sect founder/trainer

**Status:** Imprisoned (15+ years)

**Background:**
- Ceremonial magic purist
- Killed unlicensed half-dragons trying to pass as human
- Created magical sword through ritual murders (never recovered)
- Trained Mal for unknown purpose (revealed Book 5)
- Part of or founder of dark sect ideology

**Ideology:**
- Half-dragons are "mistakes" from non-traditional magic
- Proper response: "cleanup" (murder) and harvesting their magic
- Trained others in her techniques (how many?)

**Legacy:**
- Sword still missing (everyone searching)
- Trained Mal with extensive ritual knowledge
- Sect wants to complete her work
- Copycat uses her exact methods
- Her techniques recognizable in sect enchantments

**Impact on Mal:**
- Source of trauma and repressed memories
- Rejection of magical practice stems from her
- Professional expertise comes from her training
- Fear of becoming her drives choices
- Must face her legacy to stop sect

**Not Redeemed:**
- Genuinely believed half-dragons were property/abominations
- No redemption arc
- Mal must accept: mother was wrong, choices were evil
- Breaking cycle, not understanding/forgiving

### Criminal Kingpin Oz

**Role:** Jax's uncle, runs underground protection network, Church connections

**Status:** Half-dragon, criminal operations, under investigation

**Background:**
- Half-dragon operating in underground economy
- Runs protection racket for unlicensed half-dragons
- Pays Church tithe for foster family's preservation
- Maintains Church connections through bribes AND blackmail

**Operations:**
- Provides: fake IDs, safe houses, employment
- Protects half-dragons escaping Guild control
- Tracks sect activity (protecting his clients/assets)
- Has information network (how he gets intel)

**Church Connections:**
- Pays tithe for foster family (expensive but effective)
- Has blackmail material on Church officials
- Provides "discreet services" (maintains leverage)
- Church blocks warrants, withholds evidence for him

**Moral Complexity:**
- Criminal kingpin (exploitation, illegal operations)
- BUT helps Guild's victims survive
- Protects clients from sect
- Helps stop Rite of Ascension (Book 5)

**Investigation Status:**
- Simone and Eliot building off-books case
- Solid evidence but can't move (Church protection)
- Helped stop sect (complicates prosecution)
- Unresolved by Series 1 end

**Relationships:**
- **Jax:** Nephew; complicated loyalty
- **Lark:** Son; memory-alteration magic user
- **Church officials:** Mutually beneficial relationships
- **Half-dragon community:** Protection provider
- **Simone/Eliot:** Being investigated

- Mother's ritual techniques (theoretical knowledge)

- Does NOT know: sword location, mother's true purpose for her

**Book 5:**
- Everything from Book 1
- Sect structure and ideology
- Her mother trained others

- What she was being trained for

**Book 1:**
- Mal is The Darkness's daughter
- Has ritual expertise
- Sword never recovered
- Suspects Mal has it/knows location

**Book 5:**
- Mal turned mother in (victim, not accomplice)

**Throughout Series:**
