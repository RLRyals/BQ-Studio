### MARKET GAPS & OPPORTUNITIES

**üí° HIGH-VALUE OPPORTUNITIES:**

1. **Wonka Retelling Gap**
   - **Gap:** No major dark Wonka retellings in adult romance space (some YA, nothing adult gothic romance)
   - **Opportunity:** First-mover advantage in specific niche
   - **Risk:** Lower than fairy tale (Wonka less universally known than Cinderella)
   - **Mitigation:** Frame as "mad inventor's wonderland" not explicitly "Wonka retelling" for broader appeal

2. **Beauty-IS-the-Beast + Steampunk**
   - **Gap:** Her Dark Promise doing beauty-as-beast in fantasy, but not steampunk industrial
   - **Opportunity:** Combine trending role-reversal with trending steampunk aesthetic
   - **Unique Angle:** Daughter is the monster, father is the "keeper," protagonist must navigate both

3. **Serial Killer Romance in Historical Setting**
   - **Gap:** Most serial killer romances are contemporary; few in Victorian/steampunk
   - **Opportunity:** Combine "serial killer who won't kill you" trope with Victorian constraints
   - **Unique Angle:** She's locked up not just for being dangerous, but because Victorian society would institutionalize/execute her

4. **Steampunk Gothic Factory Setting**
   - **Gap:** Most steampunk is airships/cities; few factory-as-wonderland settings
   - **Opportunity:** Willy Wonka's factory is iconic; make it gothic and deadly
   - **Unique Angle:** Candy factory becomes torture chamber, whimsy becomes horror

### RECOMMENDED SERIES STRUCTURE

**5-BOOK SERIES: YES - Highly Recommended**

**Why 5 Books:**
- Complex mythology (father's inventions, daughter's history, factory's secrets) sustains 5 books
- Wonderland/factory has multiple "rooms" to explore (book-per-section structure)
- Character relationships need slow development (daughter trust, father reveal, protagonist growth)
- Serial killer backstory requires time to reveal and complicate
- Multiple victims/mysteries provide book-level plots within series arc

**Escalation Pattern:**
- Book 1: Discover factory, meet daughter, first death
- Book 2: Uncover father's experiments, daughter's history
- Book 3: Protagonist complicit, must choose sides
- Book 4: Factory's true purpose revealed, apocalyptic stakes
- Book 5: Destroy factory or save daughter, impossible choice
