## Final Thoughts

**Remember:**
- Style serves story and reader, not ego
- Genre conventions exist for good reasons (reader expectations)
- You can have unique voice within genre boundaries
- The goal is to make reading effortless and enjoyable

**Your voice matters:**
Don't lose your unique perspective while following genre conventions. The best books honor genre expectations while bringing something fresh.

**Keep learning:**
Read widely in your genre. Study what works. Notice patterns. Update this guide as you learn.
