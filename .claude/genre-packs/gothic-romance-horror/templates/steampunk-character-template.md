**The Ghost (Inciting Wound):**
What traumatic event shaped who they are today?
- [Example: Watched father die in workshop explosion | Was transformed against will | Lost everything when invention failed | Accidentally killed someone with creation]

**The Lie/Misbelief:**

- [Example: "I destroy everything I touch" | "Love is weakness that causes mistakes" | "I'm no longer human enough to deserve happiness" | "Progress matters more than people"]

**The Need (Psychological):**

**The Want (External Goal):**

- [Example: Perfect the invention that killed their mentor | Become fully mechanical to eliminate weakness | Earn respect of scientific community | Restore their fortune/reputation]

## Usage Notes

This template is designed for comprehensive character development in steampunk gothic horror romance. Not every field must be filled for every characterâ€”choose the elements that serve your story.

**For Protagonists:** Fill out extensively, especially psychology, wounds, and arc.
**For Love Interests:** Focus on background, secrets, and relationship dynamics.
**For Supporting Characters:** Select relevant sections only.
**For Antagonists:** Emphasize motivations, methods, and connection to protagonist.

The more you develop now, the less you'll struggle with consistency later.
