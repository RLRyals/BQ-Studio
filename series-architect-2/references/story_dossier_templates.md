# Story Dossier Templates (Curated)

Use these starting points based on your genre and planning style. Mix and match sections as needed.

## Book-Level (All-in-One)
- `templates/book_dossier_template.md` — Comprehensive dossier combining overview, beats, chapters, scenes, and subplots.

## Story Dossier Worksheets
- `templates/story_dossier_worksheet_genre_neutral.md` — Broad, genre-neutral worksheet for most stories.
- `templates/story_dossier_worksheet_truby_style.md` — Truby-flavored worksheet emphasizing moral argument and character web.

## Character & Development Aids
- `templates/character_architecture_template.md` — Deep character design.
- `templates/chapter_summary_template.md` — Chapter-by-chapter planning.
- `templates/scene_card_template.md` — Granular scene planning.
- `templates/subplot_tracking_template.md` — Track subplots and continuity.

## Series-Level (Optional)
- `templates/series_bible_template.md` — Comprehensive series documentation.
- `templates/series_overview_template.md` — Lightweight series scaffold.

Tip: Start simple. Add sections only when they unlock clarity.
