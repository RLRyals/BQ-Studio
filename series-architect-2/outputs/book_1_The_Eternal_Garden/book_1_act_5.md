## Chapter 23: Dual POV - "The Proof" (~4,500 words)

### Major Events

2. **The demonstration**: Create video recording (pneumatic camera) of partial cure effect
3. **Editing strategy**: Show resonance apparatus working (brief), hide catastrophic failure
4. **Augustus as messenger**: Will deliver documentation to Court on Celeste's behalf (proxy)
5. **The risk**: Court may not accept proxy; may demand Celeste's presence anyway
6. **Research breakthrough** (during preparation):
   - Margot reviewing failure data footage
   - Spots pattern Celeste missed: Resonance worked briefly BEFORE inverting
   - **New theory**: Cure failed because purely mechanical; needs biological catalyst component
   - Combination approach: Counter-resonance + biological/alchemical element
7. **The hope**: Second attempt could work with new approach (hybrid mechanical-biological)
8. **Shared excitement**: Both energized by breakthrough (intellectual partnership at peak)
9. Augustus departs for Court with sealed documentation
10. **Chapter End**: Waiting for Court's response; planning second cure attempt

**Cure Research Progress**:

- **New theory**: Counter-resonance + biological catalyst = success possible
- **Second attempt setup**: Book 2 will test hybrid approach
- **Success rate estimate**: Could rise from 0.3% to 5-10% (Celeste's calculation)
