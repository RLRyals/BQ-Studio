## Chapter 11: Margot's POV - "The Supply Chain" (~3,500 words)

### Major Events
1. Week 6, Margot insists on understanding blood supply logistics
2. Augustus explains system: Hospital waste, morgue contacts, animal blood experiments
3. Victorian context: Body trade, medical waste disposal, class system enables secrecy
4. The network: How Augustus maintains supply without suspicion
5. Margot's horror: She's helping sustain a vampire
6. Margot's rationalization: "She's not killing anyone. I'm preventing murders."
7. **The slippery slope**: First conscious choice to help beyond self-preservation
8. Margot helps Augustus unload blood shipment
9. **Chapter End**: Margot sees herself in mirror; doesn't recognize reflection

- Class system enables secrecy (street urchins not missed)

**Emotional Beats**:
- Margot's first major boundary crossing
- Rationalization: "I'm preventing worse"
- Self-awareness of corruption

**Complicity Marker #1**: Active participation in blood supply

**Power Dynamic Shift**: 90/10 → 80/20 (Margot taking initiative)

## Chapter 12: Celeste's POV - "The Gratitude" (~3,500 words)

### Major Events
1. Week 7, Celeste trying to express appreciation
2. The gift: Celeste builds Margot a mechanical nightingale (intricate, beautiful)
3. Celeste's confusion: Doesn't understand her own motivation (why gift?)
4. Margot's reaction: Touched despite herself, then angry at being touched
5. Conversation: Margot asks about Celeste's victims (pre-vampire murders)
6. Celeste's answer: Clinical details, no remorse, genuine confusion at Margot's horror
7. **Key exchange**:
   - Margot: "Don't you feel anything for them?"
   - Celeste: "I feel curious. Isn't that a feeling?"
8. Margot realizes: Celeste truly is psychopath; attraction to brilliance doesn't change that
9. **Chapter End**: Margot keeps the nightingale (despite everything)

**Character Development**:
- Celeste: First attempt at gratitude/gift-giving (novel emotion)
- Margot: Recognizes psychopathy clearly, but stays anyway

**Emotional Beats**:
- Beauty (nightingale) intertwined with horror (made by killer's hands)
- Margot's self-awareness: Knows what Celeste is, stays anyway

## Chapter 15: Dual POV - "The Experiment" (~4,000 words)

### MIDPOINT - Part 1: Hope → Terror

### Major Events
1. Day of first cure attempt, Week 9
2. Setup: Celeste strapped into resonance apparatus
3. Process: Mechanical vibrations at calculated counter-resonance frequency
4. Margot's role: Monitoring vitals, adjusting settings
5. Initial signs: Seems to be working (vampire traits diminishing)
6. **The hope**: Both allowing themselves to believe
7. **MIDPOINT TURN**: Apparatus malfunctions; resonance inverts
8. **The amplification**: Celeste's vampire traits amplify instead of diminish
9. **The horror**: Eyes fully black, fangs extend, strength doubles, becomes MORE vampiric
10. Restraints break; Celeste free and feral
11. **Cliffhanger**: Feral Celeste advancing on Margot

**Cure Attempt #1**:
- Method: Simple counter-resonance via mechanical vibrations
- Result: SPECTACULAR FAILURE - inverted resonance amplifies traits
- Success Rate: 0.3%
- Learning: Vampirism more complex than simple resonance disruption

**Emotional Beats**:
- Hope building → working! → malfunction → horror → terror
- Midpoint turn: Hope → Despair

## Chapter 16: Margot's POV - "The Hunt" (~5,000 words)

### MIDPOINT - Part 2: Survival

### Major Events
1. Immediately following Ch 15: Celeste hunting Margot through Gardens
2. **The chase**: Suspense sequence, Margot using knowledge of systems to evade
3. Celeste's state: Feral but still intelligent (worst combination)
4. Near-death moments: Multiple close calls, Margot injured (cut, bruised)
5. **The trap**: Margot lures Celeste into resonance chamber, reverses apparatus
6. **The reversal**: Celeste returns to baseline vampire state, collapses
7. **Aftermath**: Celeste unconscious; Margot bleeding; apparatus destroyed
8. Hours pass: Margot tending wounds, watching Celeste unconscious
9. Celeste wakes: Remembers everything, horrified at what she nearly did
10. **Chapter End**: Celeste's first words: "I'm sorry" (doesn't know how to apologize)

**Action Sequence**:
- Margot runs through Gardens (Level 1 → Level 2 → back)
- Uses pneumatic systems (steam bursts, pressure locks)
- Leverages knowledge Celeste taught her
- Injured but survives

**Emotional Beats**:
- Terror, survival instinct, betrayal
- Margot realizes: Danger was always real, just hidden
- Celeste: First experience of guilt-like emotion

**Power Dynamic Shift**: 75/25 → 100/0 (danger reasserted)
